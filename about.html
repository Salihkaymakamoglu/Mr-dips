About Us

We Are Mr.Dips

Mr. Dips is leading producer of Mediterranean dips and salads, we also specialise in strained yogurt and many other dairy products. Mr Dips manufacturers all of its products in a modern site, operated in accordance with HACCP and ISO9001 guidelines to ensure the highest quality reaches its customers. All ingredients are specially sourced and imported to meet our high quality standards. We supply a huge variety to all consumers being supermarkets, hotels, wholesalers and restaurants. With over 14 years experience Mr Dips is always our mission is to continually improve our products and services to meet our customers needs...
